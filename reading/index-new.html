Reading to Stay Sane

The crazier the news gets, the more I find myself needing a meticulously researched, wise book to keep my brain from spiralling in anxiety. This isn’t a comprehensive list of what you should read to be A Good Citizen, though I do think reading helps with that. This is simply a recreation of the list that lives on my phone, and as such overrepresents my particular interests in feminism, science, and writing. If that’s your thing, I hope you’ll read along with me, and send recs!
